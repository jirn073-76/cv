Short CV

In 2021 I finished my diploma in Computer Science and Software Engineering at HTL Donaustadt with highest distinction. Afterwards, I started working in Investment Banking / QFin at RCB, developing tools for Structuring & Trading while studying Business Informatics at the University of Applied Sciences Technikum Vienna. This is where I did a study abroad programme at the Iowa State University of Science and Technology (Ames, USA), where I majored in Management Information Systems at the Ivy College of Business and got placed on the 2022 Dean's List.

After returning at the start of 2023, I started work as a Software Engineer at IBM where I got involved developing AI Solutions. In the same year I enrolled in the Master's Programmes for Software Engineering at UAS Technikum (Vienna, Austria) and Computer Science at Università degli Studi Guglielmo Marconi (Rome, Italy).

In 2024 I started my work for the City of Vienna at the Magistrate's Finance Department (MA 5). During this time I enrolled in the Master's Programme for Information and Media Law at the University of Vienna's Faculty of Law (Juridicum).
